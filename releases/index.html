Governance rule for old releases

Old monthly folders should be treated as immutable public artifacts. New releases should add new folders under ProdIP_Files instead of replacing or renaming earlier release folders.

  • Keep March 2026 under Export/release_2026-03/.
  • Publish April 2026 under Export/release_2026-04/.
  • Add future months under their own release_YYYY-MM folder.
