Methodology

Geolocation precision

How country, region, city, and confidence-style precision signals are represented in the monthly dataset.

Precision levels

Geolocation records can be represented at several levels of precision:

  • City: city-level GeoName and coordinates are available.
  • Region: region or state-level attribution is available.
  • Country: only country-level attribution is available.
  • Unknown: no reliable location hierarchy is available.

Accuracy radius

The accuracy_radius_km field provides an approximate radius for the location. Smaller values indicate more specific attribution.

Coordinates

Latitude and longitude should be interpreted together with geo_precision_level and accuracy_radius_km. A coordinate is not a street-level claim.

Recommended interpretation

Use country-level fields for broad classification, region/city fields for enrichment, and precision fields to decide whether a record is suitable for high-confidence analysis.
