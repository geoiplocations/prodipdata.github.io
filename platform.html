Platform

GeoIP data built for practical use.

GeoIP Locations is the public data experience of ProdIPData. It helps teams explore country coverage, ASN structure, registry footprint, and downloadable files that can be used in analytics, routing, applications, and operational workflows.

Explore the interactive country coverage map, open the monthly GeoIP download indexes, browse the release archive, open the current release overview, or review the ProdIPData methodology to understand how each release is assembled.

Downloadable GeoIP data

Country profiles, ASN views, registry summaries, and downloadable file catalogs are published in formats that fit local analysis, reporting, and system integration.

Reliable static delivery

The public website is intentionally static. That keeps access simple, reduces operational risk, and lets teams review the published data without depending on a live production database.

Monthly refresh cycle

The platform is refreshed on a monthly cadence so users can work from a consistent release snapshot across the website, downloadable files, and supporting reference material.

What the platform supports

  • Country-by-country coverage views and global summaries that help users understand where published internet footprint is concentrated.
  • ASN and registry insights that make it easier to review network ownership, leading operators, and market structure.
  • Downloadable CSV and Parquet files that can be hosted locally and integrated into internal tools, analytics flows, and data pipelines.
  • Browser-based tables, charts, and maps that help teams compare countries, registries, and network composition without extra tooling.

ProdIPData files

ProdIPData files are designed for teams that want to download GeoIP data, host it locally, and integrate it into their own reporting, applications, or operational workflows. Monthly CSV and Parquet packages make it easier to move from website exploration to direct internal use.

  • Access monthly release files through dedicated HTML package indexes.
  • Review metadata such as snapshot month, package structure, and published footprint counts.
  • Use files for internal analytics, routing reviews, enrichment jobs, and downstream data preparation.

Beyond geolocation

GeoIP is not only about approximate location. ProdIPData extends the picture with ASN, registry, and market-structure views so users can understand who operates the visible footprint, how concentrated a country may be, and how coverage is distributed.

  • Country and registry views help explain overall scale and regional distribution.
  • ASN panels and rankings provide operator context beyond simple location labels.
  • Concentration and composition views support more informed interpretation of the published internet footprint.
