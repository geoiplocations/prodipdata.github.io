ASN data by country

ASN data by country for footprint, ownership, and concentration analysis.

Country-level ASN analysis helps teams move beyond simple location labels. ProdIPData highlights how many ASNs appear in each country, which network leads the visible footprint, and how concentrated or diversified the published structure may be.

Use the coverage page for the interactive map, the downloads hub for release files, or the GeoIP database overview for a higher-level product view.

Country footprint review

Compare countries by published /24 footprint, IPv4 estimate, ASN count, and registry alignment to understand relative scale in the current release.

Lead ASN share

Lead ASN share shows how much of the visible footprint is controlled by the dominant network in a country. It is a practical indicator of concentration and market structure.

Registry context

Country views can be grouped by regional registry to show how footprint and operator presence are distributed across ARIN, RIPE, APNIC, AFRINIC, and LACNIC.

What users typically look for

  • Countries with the largest visible network footprint in the current release.
  • Countries where one network dominates the published footprint versus countries where the structure is more distributed.
  • Regional comparisons that show how country portfolios differ across the five regional internet registries.
  • Country-level composition that can be exported or combined with other internal workflows.
